Hey, I'm Hassan 👋

Starting late doesn't mean finishing last.

I spent years watching "leadership" become a synonym for "popularity" — people getting handed positions because they knew the right people, not because they were qualified. Titles traded like favors among friends in school leadership rather than earned through merit. I decided I didn't want that kind of leadership. So I skipped the system entirely and started something from nothing. As the Founder of SetBuddy AI, I vetted over 70 applicants to build a 14-role team across four departments. I didn't hire based on social fit or how long I had known a candidate; I hired strictly on qualification, assembling a team of specialists who were often more experienced in their niches than I was. I didn't want a title granted by a faculty advisor. I wanted the sleepless nights, the hard calls, and a product that lives on the Google Play Store — not on a school bulletin board. The gym is where it started — I was tired of second-guessing my own form, so I built an app that checks it for you.
